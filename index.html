Our Story

Where Heritage Meets Luxury

Nestled on the enchanting island of Djerba, Dar Sawani is a boutique guesthouse that embodies the timeless elegance of Tunisian hospitality. Our establishment seamlessly blends traditional architecture with contemporary luxury, offering an unforgettable experience for discerning travelers.

Originally built in the 19th century, Dar Sawani has been meticulously restored to preserve its authentic charm while incorporating modern amenities. Each room and suite has been thoughtfully designed to provide the utmost comfort and sophistication.

Our dedicated team is committed to providing personalized service that exceeds expectations, ensuring every guest enjoys a truly memorable stay in paradise.
